A little bit about me 🤠


Hi there! I'm Andy and I am an interdisciplinary product designer and developer based in Boston and New Hampshire. I will be graduating from Boston University and the Kilachand Honors College with a Bachelor's in Computer Science.


Through my experiences, I am inspired by social innovation and storytelling. To bring people together through accessible design is the goal of every project I pursue. At the heart of UX design is to maximize the "naturalness" of the interactions between a user and the product. How can we make the user flow not only fun and not robotic but also natural like a conversation? It's all about adding a bit more color to the world.


Outside of Figma, I am an avid tree climber 🌲, major foodie (cooking and eating) 🍜, Star Wars enthusiast 🌌, and indie rock/pop guitarist 🎸. With my headset in and music on, I'll be dancing in my room or heel clicking down the street trying to beat my 89,119 minutes of Spotify from last year.


But enough about me. Let's talk about you! Message me through email or LinkedIn with some food recommendations and let's have ourselves a coffee chat!
